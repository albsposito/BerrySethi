Berry-Sethi Algorithm

The Berry-Sethi algorithm is a technique used to convert regular expressions into nondeterministic finite automata (NFA). It constructs the automaton in a bottom-up manner by parsing the regular expression and applying rules for each type of operator. This algorithm is efficient and forms the basis for tools like lexical analyzers in compilers.
